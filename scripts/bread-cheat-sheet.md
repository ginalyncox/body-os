# 🍞 Bread Cheat Sheet

**Seated workflow · saddle stool · wet hands only · wild yeast safe**

---

## ⏱ 1-Minute Reset (before you start)

1. Sit. Feet supported.
2. Hands palm-down on thighs.
3. **Breath:** double inhale through nose, long exhale through mouth. **×4**
4. **Say out loud:** *"I'm being gentle with this. The dough is patient."*
5. Sip water.

→ Now you can bake.

---

## 🧂 Ingredients

| Item | Amount |
| :--- | ---: |
| Bread flour | **500 g** |
| Lukewarm water | **350 g** |
| Active starter | **100 g** |
| Salt | **10 g** |

---

## 🛠 Tools (lay out first)

- Saddle stool
- Big bowl
- **Silicone spatula** (starter feeds, scraping)
- **Long wooden spoon** (mixing dough)
- Kitchen scale
- Banneton or towel-lined bowl
- Dutch oven + lid
- Long oven mitts
- Sharp blade / razor (scoring)
- Damp folded towel (spatula wipe)
- Water glass (for you)
- **Empty sheet pan** ← for the rack below the dutch oven

---

## 📋 Day 0 — Feed Starter (5 min, evening)

1. Stir starter with spatula.
2. Discard to ~50 g.
3. Add **50 g flour + 50 g water** straight from containers.
4. Stir. Loose lid. Back in 78°F box.
5. Rinse spatula.

---

## 📋 Day 1 — Mix (5 min, morning)

1. **Water** into bowl.
2. **Starter** in with spatula. Swirl with wooden spoon.
3. **Salt** in. Swirl.
4. **Flour** poured from container. Stir until shaggy.
5. Cover. Walk away.

---

## 📋 Day 1 — Folds (30 sec × 3–4 rounds)

Every couple hours, when you're up:

1. **Wet both hands** under lukewarm tap.
2. Grab edge, stretch up, fold to center.
3. Rotate bowl ¼ turn. Repeat ×3 more.
4. **Rinse hands immediately.**
5. Cover. Walk away.

---

## 📋 Day 1 — Shape (3 min, evening)

1. Lightly flour counter (pour, never scoop).
2. Wet hands. Turn dough out.
3. Pull 4 sides into center. Flip seam-side down.
4. Into floured banneton. Cover.
5. **Fridge overnight (8–24 hrs).**

---

## 🔥 Day 2 — Bake (your verified recipe)

1. **Preheat dutch oven inside oven to 500°F.**
2. **Put empty sheet pan on rack below.** ← prevents dark bottom
3. Dough out of fridge → onto parchment.
4. One confident slash with the blade.
5. Lift in. Lid on.
6. **500°F · 20 min · lid on**
7. **450°F · 18 min · lid off**
8. Cool on rack **30 minutes minimum** before slicing.

---

## ⚠️ Skin Safety Rules

- **Wet hand first, every time.** Wet skin doesn't grab dough.
- **Rinse immediately** after dough contact — never let dough-water dry on skin.
- **Pour flour** — never scoop with bare hands.
- **No commercial yeast** ever. Wild starter only.
- **Lukewarm water** — never cold tap, never hot.

---

## 🟢🟡🔴 Day Check Before You Start

- **Green day?** Full workflow.
- **Yellow day?** Wet spatula for folds instead of bare hands. Skip shaping — dump into banneton. Bake later.
- **Red day?** Fridge the dough. It waits 2–3 days cold without harm.

**The fridge is the pause button. Always.**

---

## 🧯 If Something Feels Off

- Pain climbing → cover dough, dismount, lie down.
- Hijacked feeling → run reset-5min, dough goes in fridge.
- Anxious second-guessing → trust the bread. The husband test counts.
