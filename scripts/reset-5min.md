# Script: reset-5min

*Standard mid-flare reset. Use for severity 5–7 or after a doom loop / sensory overload.*

## Trigger condition
- Moderate flare (severity 5–7)
- After a runbook fires
- Mid-day mandatory checkpoint on yellow days

## Preconditions
- Seated or lying down
- Phone face-down or in another room
- One light source, low and warm

## Steps (5 minutes total)
1. **Minute 1 — Settle.** Sit or recline. Hands palm-down on thighs or belly. Eyes soft (not closed hard).
2. **Minute 2 — Breath.** 4 in, 6 out, for the full minute. If counting is too much, just make exhales longer than inhales.
3. **Minute 3 — Body scan.** Top of head down to feet, naming what each region is doing. Do not change anything; just notice. ("Jaw is tight. Shoulders down. Right hand burning. Feet warm.")
4. **Minute 4 — One small care.** Water. Adjust temperature. Add a blanket. Dim a light. One body need met.
5. **Minute 5 — Reentry.** Decide: continue current activity, switch to lower load, or escalate to reset-deep. Say the choice out loud.

## Success criteria
- Heart rate visibly slower.
- One small need has been met.
- You can articulate the choice to continue, switch, or stop without second-guessing.

## Fallback
If still at severity 7+ after the reset, escalate to `scripts/reset-deep.md`.
