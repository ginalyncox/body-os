# Script: reset-60s

*Fastest recovery script. Use for any flare 1–4, or as a preemptive break between work blocks.*

## Trigger condition
- Mild flare detected (severity ≤ 4)
- Transition between tasks or contexts
- Anytime, no permission needed

## Preconditions
- Seated. If standing, sit first.
- Hands rested on thighs, not gripping.

## Steps (60 seconds total)
1. **Physiological sigh × 4.** Double inhale through nose, long exhale through mouth. ~30 seconds.
2. **Name three things felt.** Fabric, breath, seat. Out loud or silently. ~15 seconds.
3. **One sentence:** *"My nervous system is doing a lot. I am being gentle with it."* ~5 seconds.
4. **Sip water.** ~10 seconds.

## Success criteria
- A natural exhale or yawn happens.
- You can name what you were doing before the reset without confusion.

## Fallback
If the flare hasn't softened, escalate to `scripts/reset-5min.md`.
