# Script: reset-deep

*Long-form recovery. Use for severe flares (8+), end-of-yellow-day wind-down, or after any runbook for Hijacked / Pain Spike at 7+.*

## Trigger condition
- Severity 8–10 flare
- Two failed reset-5min attempts
- Mandatory before bed on red or yellow days

## Preconditions
- Bed, couch, or floor with support
- Low light or darkness
- Sound: brown noise, a familiar low-stim show, or silence — whichever the body wants
- Phone in another room or on airplane mode
- No expectation to "finish" anything tonight

## Steps (20–60 minutes)
1. **Body horizontal.** Knees supported by a pillow if helpful. Hands on belly.
2. **Long exhale breath, 5 min.** 4 in, 8 out. Do not force; if shorter is what's available, shorter is fine.
3. **Temperature regulation.** Blanket on. Socks if feet are cold. Damp cool cloth on forehead if the spike is hot.
4. **Weighted blanket or compression** if it feels good. Remove immediately if it makes anything worse.
5. **No content.** No reading, no scrolling. Sound only, or silence. The brain needs to stop processing input.
6. **Stay** for at least 20 minutes, even if you feel better at 10. The autonomic system has hysteresis — it can drop and re-spike without enough downtime.
7. **Reentry decision.** Choose: sleep, continue resting, or stand up slowly and resume *one* low-load activity. No work.

## Success criteria
- The body feels heavier (parasympathetic engagement).
- A natural deep sigh or yawn.
- Pain or autonomic activation drops at least 2 points.
- You can lie still without urgency.

## Fallback
If no improvement after 30 minutes, drop to Black protocol. Sleep if it comes. Notify a trusted person if needed.
