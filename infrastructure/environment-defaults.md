# Environment Defaults

*Baseline environmental settings that keep the autonomic system closer to neutral.*

## Lighting
- Overhead lights off when possible. Use lamps at counter height instead.
- Warm color temperature (2700–3000K).
- Dimmer below 50% after 8 PM.
- One "reset" lamp in every workspace — single tap, low warm light, comfort default.

## Sound
- Background sound is better than silence (silence amplifies internal noise like tinnitus, pulse).
- Brown noise or fan at low volume is the safe default.
- No sudden notifications. Phone on Focus or Do Not Disturb during work blocks.
- One pair of low-pressure earplugs (Loop Quiet or equivalent) always within reach.

## Temperature
- Ambient room temp 68–72°F.
- Lukewarm water for hands, dishes, drinking (cold and hot both flare).
- A throw blanket within reach of every seated workstation.
- No barefoot on cold floors. Slippers or socks always.

## Seating
- Saddle stool in the kitchen (deployed).
- Office chair with foot support and lumbar.
- Couch with a wedge pillow option.
- Bed with a backrest pillow for upright resting.
- **Rule: feet always supported. No dangling.**

## Sensory hygiene
- Phone face-down during meals and resets.
- One screen at a time when symptomatic.
- Visual clutter on the counter triggers cognitive load — keep one clear zone always.

## Hydration and fuel defaults
- Water glass within arm's reach at every seated station.
- Pre-portioned snacks where they're needed (kitchen, desk, bedside).
- Lukewarm water bottle by the bed.

## When in doubt
Reduce one input. Dim a light, lower a sound, add a layer, sit down. Subtraction is the default move.
