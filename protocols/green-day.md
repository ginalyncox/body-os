# Protocol: Green Day

*Low symptoms. Regulated. Body has bandwidth.*

## Entry criteria
- Morning pain ≤ 3/10
- Morning energy ≥ 6/10
- Slept ≥ 6 hours
- Autonomic state: Calm

## What's allowed
- Full work blocks (deep code, infrastructure changes, AI agent orchestration)
- Cooking projects including bread (full sourdough workflow)
- Errands and in-person interactions
- Creative work (tarot, game design, writing)
- Exercise / movement (within tolerance)

## What's forbidden
- Pushing past 7/10 effort. Green days end early if respected; they collapse into red days if overspent.
- Skipping meals or water to keep working.
- Taking on debt against tomorrow ("I'll rest tomorrow" — no).

## Minimum daily completes
- 3 meals
- 64 oz water
- Meds
- One Daily Vitals log
- One 5-min reset midday, even if not flaring

## Exit criteria (drop to yellow)
- Symptom severity climbs to 5+ for > 30 min
- Two consecutive misses of the midday reset
- Energy drops below 4 by afternoon

## Notes
Green days are for *steady* output, not heroics. Heroics on green days create red days.
