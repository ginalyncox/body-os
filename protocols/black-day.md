# Protocol: Black Day

*System down. Survival floor only. Permission granted, in advance, by past-you.*

## Entry criteria
- Symptom severity ≥ 9 sustained
- Unable to complete basic minimums
- Two scripts have failed to reach success criteria
- Or: you know it. The body knows it.

## What's allowed
- Lying down
- Sleep, whenever it comes
- Sips of water
- Small bites of safe food
- One trusted person notified if helpful ("I'm having a black day, I'm safe, I'll be quiet today")
- Crisis line if anything tips toward unsafe — 988 (call or text)

## What's forbidden
- Everything else. Including this document, except for the survival floor.

## Minimum daily completes (the floor)
- Sips of water through the day
- One dose of any required meds
- Use the bathroom
- Stay safe

## Exit criteria
- The day ends. That's the only exit. Tomorrow may still be red. That is acceptable.

## Mandatory follow-up
- Within 72 hours of returning to yellow or green: postmortem. Even one paragraph. What led here, what was the earliest signal that got missed, what could past-you have done differently.

## Notes
**Black days exist on purpose, written in advance, so present-you doesn't have to negotiate them during a crisis.** Past-Gina has already decided: today, you don't have to function. The world will hold for one day. Your body will not.
