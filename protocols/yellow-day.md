# Protocol: Yellow Day

*Moderate symptoms. Manageable. Body is sending signals — listen.*

## Entry criteria
- Morning pain 4–6/10
- Morning energy 3–5/10
- Sleep was poor or insufficient
- Autonomic state: Edgy or Activated

## What's allowed
- Shallow work (email, code review, light admin)
- One short errand if essential
- Seated kitchen work (sourdough folds OK, no shaping marathons)
- Light reading / passive media
- Connecting with one trusted person if it doesn't drain

## What's forbidden
- Deep work blocks > 90 min without a 5-min reset
- Infrastructure deploys or anything irreversible (Git pushes to main, payments, contracts)
- Standing kitchen sessions
- Multitasking across more than 2 contexts
- New commitments to other people

## Minimum daily completes
- 3 meals (even if small)
- 64 oz water
- Meds
- One Daily Vitals log
- Two 5-min resets (mid-morning and mid-afternoon)
- One full reset-deep before bed

## Exit criteria
- **Up to Green:** symptoms drop to ≤ 3/10 for 2+ hours and energy climbs.
- **Down to Red:** symptom severity hits 7+ OR autonomic state becomes Hijacked OR a runbook fires twice in 4 hours.

## Notes
Yellow days are the most dangerous tier — the temptation is to act like it's green. Treat yellow as "work from the saddle stool, mentally and literally."
